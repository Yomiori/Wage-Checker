Our Mission

At SalaryCheck.us, we believe every professional deserves to know their true market value. Our mission is to democratize access to reliable salary information, empowering workers to make informed career decisions and negotiate fair compensation.

We leverage official government data from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics to provide accurate, unbiased salary comparisons that help bridge the information gap between employers and employees.

Why We Built This Tool

Salary transparency has been a persistent challenge in the professional world. Many employees work for years without knowing if they're being compensated fairly for their skills and experience.

We created SalaryCheck.us to solve this problem by providing:

  • Government-backed data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • Experience-level analysis for accurate comparisons
  • Location-based insights accounting for regional differences
  • Free access to professional salary information

Our Data Sources

We exclusively use official government data to ensure accuracy and reliability:

  • U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) - Primary source for salary data
  • Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics (OEWS) - Detailed wage information
  • Current Population Survey (CPS) - Employment and earnings data
  • American Community Survey (ACS) - Demographic and economic data

This government data represents real wages paid to employees across industries and is updated annually to reflect current market conditions.

Our Commitment

  • Privacy - Protecting user information and maintaining confidentiality

How We Help

SalaryCheck.us helps professionals in multiple ways:

  • Salary Negotiation - Arm yourself with objective data for compensation discussions
  • Career Planning - Understand earning potential across experience levels
